# 🥛 Milk with Meals

| Region | Custom | Offense Level | Notes |
|--------|--------|---------------|-------|
| USA/UK | Common with meals | None | Especially with cookies, cereal |
| India | Common (lassi, chai) | None | Milk-based drinks popular |
| Japan | Rare during meals | Low | Milk with breakfast only |
| China | Rare with savory meals | Low | Milk with breakfast |
| Europe (general) | Common | None | Germany, Netherlands, Scandinavia |
| Middle East | Ayran, laban common | None | Yogurt-based drinks |
| Korea | Milk with meals rare | Low | Soju/makgeolli more common |
| Africa (many) | Goat/camel milk common | None | Regional variation |
| South America | Rare with dinner | Low | More breakfast/dessert |

## Cultural Context

- **Lactose intolerance**: 70-90% of East Asians, 60-70% of Africans
- **Dairy culture**: Northern Europe, USA, India have strong dairy traditions
- **Yogurt drinks**: Middle East, Turkey, Central Asia prefer fermented
- **Tea/coffee culture**: East Asia, Middle East prefer hot beverages

## Safe Practices

- **Ask before ordering** milk with meals in East Asia
- **Try local beverages** instead (tea, ayran, lassi)
- **Observe what locals drink** with meals
- **Offer alternatives** when hosting international guests

## Regional Preferences

### East Asia
- Green tea, barley tea, oolong tea
- Soy milk (breakfast only)
- Avoid milk with savory food

### Middle East
- Ayran (yogurt + water + salt)
- Laban (buttermilk)
- Mint tea

### India
- Lassi (yogurt drink)
- Chai (milk tea)
- Buttermilk

### Northern Europe
- Milk common with meals
- Strong dairy tradition
- Cheese, yogurt popular
