# 🍚 Rice Etiquette

| Region | Custom | Offense Level | Notes |
|--------|--------|---------------|-------|
| Japan | Never waste rice, finish all | High (if wasted) | Rice is sacred |
| China | Finish rice, don't play with it | Medium | Waste is disrespectful |
| Korea | Don't lift bowl with chopsticks | Low | Use spoon for rice |
| Thailand | Rice is staple, finish it | Medium | Waste is frowned upon |
| India | Rice with right hand | Medium (if left hand) | Mix with curry |
| Indonesia | Don't leave rice uneaten | Medium | Waste is bad |
| Philippines | Finish rice | Low | Shows appreciation |
| USA/UK | Rice is side dish | None | May not finish |

## Key Taboos

1. **Never waste rice** (especially Japan, China)
2. **Don't stick chopsticks upright** in rice (funeral rite)
3. **Don't play with rice** (childish, wasteful)
4. **Use right hand** for rice in South/SE Asia
5. **Don't lift bowl with chopsticks** in Korea

## Chopstick Rules for Rice

- **Never** stick chopsticks upright in rice bowl
- **Never** pass rice chopstick-to-chopstick
- **Place** chopsticks on rest when not eating
- **Hold** rice bowl close to mouth (Japan, Korea)

## How to Eat Rice Properly

### Japan
- Lift bowl to mouth
- Use chopsticks to eat rice
- Finish every grain

### China
- Use chopsticks
- Finish rice
- Don't leave grains in bowl

### Korea
- Use metal spoon for rice
- Chopsticks for side dishes
- Don't lift bowl

### India
- Mix rice with curry using right hand
- Use fingers to push food
- Never use left hand
