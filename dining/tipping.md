# 💰 Tipping Customs Worldwide

| Region | Custom | Typical Amount | Notes |
|--------|--------|----------------|-------|
| USA | Expected | 15-20% | Servers rely on tips for income |
| Canada | Expected | 15-20% | Similar to USA |
| UK | Appreciated | 10-15% | Often included in bill |
| Japan | Offensive | 0% | Tipping implies poor service |
| China | Not expected | 0% | May cause confusion |
| South Korea | Not expected | 0% | Tipping can be awkward |
| France | Included (service) | 0-5% | "Service compris" means tip included |
| Germany | Round up | 5-10% | Round to nearest euro |
| Italy | Appreciated | 5-10% | Leave small change |
| Brazil | Included (10%) | 0-10% | "Gorjeta" often auto-added |
| Australia | Not expected | 0% | Servers paid living wage |
| New Zealand | Not expected | 0% | Similar to Australia |
| Middle East | Appreciated | 10-15% | Generosity valued |
| Mexico | Appreciated | 10-15% | Important for service workers |

## Tips

- Always check if service charge is already included
- When in doubt, observe what locals do
- Small tips are better than no tips in tipping cultures
- Not tipping in USA/Canada is considered very rude
