## Key Rules

1. **Cover shoulders and knees** in religious sites
2. **No shorts/sleeveless** in mosques, temples
3. **Head covering** for women in mosques
4. **Remove shoes** before entering homes, temples
5. **Business formal** in Asia, Middle East

## Religious Site Dress Code

| Site | Women | Men |
|------|-------|-----|
| Mosque | Cover head, arms, legs | Long pants, covered |
| Temple (Buddhist) | Cover shoulders, knees | Long pants, covered |
| Church | Cover shoulders, knees | Long pants |
| Synagogue | Cover head (men), modest | Long pants, kippah |

## Country-Specific Rules

### Middle East
- Cover shoulders, knees
- No tight/revealing clothing
- Women: headscarf in religious areas
- Men: long pants

### India
- Conservative dress preferred
- Cover shoulders, knees
- Traditional dress welcomed
- Avoid revealing clothing

### Japan
- Neat, conservative
- No tattoos visible (onsen)
- Business formal
- Uniformity valued

### Thailand
- Cover shoulders, knees for temples
- No shorts in temples
- Remove shoes
- Respectful dress

## Safe Practices

- **Research before visiting**
- **Pack conservative options**
- **Carry a shawl/scarf**
- **Dress modestly** as default
- **Observe locals**
