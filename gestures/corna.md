# 🤘 Corna / Horns Gesture

| Region | Meaning | Offense Level | Notes |
|--------|---------|---------------|-------|
| USA/UK | Rock on / heavy metal | None | Music culture |
| Italy | Cuckold (cuckold's horns) | High | Insult to someone's wife |
| Spain | Cuckold | High | Insulting |
| Portugal | Cuckold | High | Same as Italy/Spain |
| Brazil | Cuckold | High | Very offensive |
| France | Cuckold | High | Similar to Mediterranean |
| Japan | Rock music / cool | None | Music culture |
| China | Rock on | None | Western influence |
| Middle East | Evil eye protection | None | Cultural amulet gesture |

## Safe Alternatives

- 🤟 I love you (ASL) — thumb, index, pinky extended
- 👍 Thumbs up (safe in most regions)
- ✌️ Peace sign (palm outward)
- 🤝 Handshake
