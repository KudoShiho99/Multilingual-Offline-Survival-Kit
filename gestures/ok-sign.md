# 👌 OK Sign (Thumb and Index Finger Forming a Circle)

| Region | Meaning | Offense Level | Notes |
|--------|---------|---------------|-------|
| USA/UK | OK, good, no problem | None | Everyday usage |
| Brazil | Highly offensive (equivalent to middle finger) | Extreme | Absolutely avoid |
| Japan | Money | None | Common in business contexts |
| France | Zero, worthless | Low | Context-dependent |
| Turkey | Implies homosexuality | High | Avoid |
| Germany | Asshole (Arschloch) | High | Insulting |
| Italy | Worthless / zero | Medium | Similar to France |
| China | Zero / nothing | None | Common in counting |
| India | Good / OK | None | Widely understood |
| Middle East | Offensive (equivalent to sexual gesture) | High | Avoid |

## Safe Alternatives

- 👍 Thumbs up (safe in most regions, note exceptions in Middle East)
- Nodding head (safe in most cultures, though head movements vary)
- Verbal confirmation ("OK", "Good", "Understood")
