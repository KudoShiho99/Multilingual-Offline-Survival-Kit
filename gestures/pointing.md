# 👆 Pointing with Index Finger

| Region | Meaning | Offense Level | Notes |
|--------|---------|---------------|-------|
| USA/UK | Pointing, directing attention | Low | Common but can be rude |
| China | Pointing at someone is rude | Medium | Use open hand instead |
| Japan | Very rude to point at people | High | Use whole hand |
| India | Pointing at people is offensive | High | Use thumb or open hand |
| Malaysia | Offensive to point at people | High | Use right thumb |
| Africa (many regions) | Rude to point at people | Medium | Use open hand |
| Europe (general) | Somewhat rude | Low | Better to use open hand |
| Middle East | Rude to point at people | High | Use right hand, palm open |

## Safe Alternatives

- 🖐️ Open hand gesture (polite in most cultures)
- Right thumb (safe in India, Malaysia, Indonesia)
- Nodding or head gesture toward direction
- Verbal direction ("over there", "this way")
