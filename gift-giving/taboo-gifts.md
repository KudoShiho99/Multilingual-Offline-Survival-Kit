# 🚫 Taboo Gifts

| Gift | Region | Offense Level | Notes |
|------|--------|---------------|-------|
| Clock (送钟) | China | Extreme | Sounds like "attending funeral" |
| White flowers | China, Japan, Korea | Extreme | Funeral color |
| Black flowers | Most | High | Funeral/mourning |
| Sharp objects | Most | Medium | "Cutting" relationship |
| Alcohol | Middle East, Muslim | Extreme | Forbidden |
| Pork products | Middle East, Muslim, Jewish | Extreme | Religious prohibition |
| Dog products | Middle East, some Asia | High | Dogs considered unclean |
| Handkerchiefs | China, Korea | Medium | Wiping tears (sadness) |
| Hats | China, some Asia | Medium | Covering head (bad luck) |
| Green hats | China | Extreme | Implies cuckoldry |
| Numbers (4) | China, Japan, Korea | High | Sounds like "death" |
| Numbers (13) | Western | Medium | Unlucky number |
| Pear (梨) | China | Medium | Sounds like "separation" |
| Umbrella (伞) | China, Japan, Korea | Medium | Sounds like "separation" |
| Red ink | China, Korea | High | Writing names in red = death |
| Tissues | China, Korea | Medium | Associated with death |
| Mirror | China, India | Medium | Bad luck, breakage |
| Empty wallet | Japan | Medium | Empty pockets = no future |
| Anything in sets of 4 | East Asia | High | Number 4 = death |

## Safe Gift Choices

| Region | Good Gifts |
|--------|------------|
| China | Red envelopes, tea, fruit, sweets |
| Japan | Sweets, sake, quality items |
| Korea | Fruit, meat, quality goods |
| Middle East | Dates, sweets, flowers (not alcohol) |
| India | Sweets, dry fruits, religious items |
| USA/UK | Wine, chocolates, flowers |
| France | Wine, cheese, chocolates |
| Brazil | Sweets, coffee, wine |

## Religious Gift Taboos

| Religion | Avoid |
|----------|-------|
| Muslim | Alcohol, pork, dog products, images |
| Jewish | Pork, shellfish, mixed meat/dairy |
| Hindu | Leather, alcohol, beef |
| Buddhist | Alcohol, weapons |
| Christian | Generally accepting |

## Safe Practices

- **Research before gifting**
- **Avoid clocks, sharp objects** universally
- **Avoid white wrapping** in Asia
- **Use both hands** to give in Asia
- **When in doubt**, give food/sweets
