## Wrapping Rules

### Japan
- Beautiful wrapping essential
- Use furoshiki (cloth wrapping)
- Present with both hands
- Don't open in front of giver

### China
- Red/gold for good luck
- Avoid white/black
- Use both hands to give
- Don't open in front of giver

### Korea
- Similar to China
- Avoid white/black
- Use both hands
- Don't open immediately

### Middle East
- Gold/green preferred
- Avoid alcohol gifts
- Use right hand
- Don't wrap with left hand

### India
- Bright colors preferred
- Avoid white (funeral)
- Use both hands
- Religious gifts appreciated

## Gift Presentation

| Region | How to Give |
|--------|-------------|
| Japan | Both hands, slight bow |
| China | Both hands, slight bow |
| Korea | Both hands, slight bow |
| India | Right hand, or both hands |
| Middle East | Right hand |
| West | One hand OK, smile |

## Safe Practices

- **Use festive colors** (red, gold, green)
- **Avoid white/black** for wrapping
- **Use both hands** to give in Asia
- **Don't open immediately** in Asia
- **Research local customs** first
