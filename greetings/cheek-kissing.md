## Key Rules

1. **Start with right cheek** (most European countries)
2. **Don't kiss strangers** (observe first)
3. **Men: be cautious** in conservative cultures
4. **Return the gesture** if initiated by local
5. **Air kiss is OK** (don't actually touch lips)

| Italy | 2 | Left → Right |
| Spain | 2 | Right → Left |
| Netherlands | 3 | Right → Left → Right |
| Belgium | 3 | Right → Left → Right |
| Brazil | 1-2 | Varies by region |
| Argentina | 1 | Right cheek |
| Russia | 2 | Cheeks alternating |

## When to Avoid

- Professional/business settings (use handshake)
- First meeting with stranger
- Conservative religious areas
- During pandemic/illness
- When person steps back

## Safe Practices

- **Observe locals** first
- **Let them initiate** cheek kissing
- **Air kiss** if unsure
- **Handshake is safe** default
- **When in doubt**, smile and nod
