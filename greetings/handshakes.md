## Key Rules

1. **Always right hand** (Muslim, Hindu, African cultures)
2. **Firm in West** (USA, Germany, Russia)
3. **Light in East** (Japan, China, Korea)
4. **Bow in Asia** (Japan, Korea, Thailand)
5. **Eye contact varies** (direct in West, avoid in Asia)

## Alternatives

- 🙏 **Namaste** (India, Nepal) - palms together, slight bow
- 🙇 **Bow** (Japan, Korea) - angle depends on status
- 🤲 **Wai** (Thailand) - prayer hands with bow
- 🫂 **Hug** (Latin America, close friends)
- 💋 **Cheek kiss** (France, Middle East, Latin America)

## When to Avoid Handshake

- Opposite gender in conservative Muslim areas
- When person has cold/illness
- During COVID-19 pandemic
- When hands are dirty (eating, working)
