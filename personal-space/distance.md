## Why It Matters

- **Too close**: Can feel threatening, invasive
- **Too far**: Can feel cold, unfriendly
- **Varies by relationship**: Friends closer than strangers
- **Varies by setting**: Crowded spaces = closer OK

## Cultural Patterns

### Close-contact cultures (0.5-0.8m)
- Latin America, Middle East, Southern Europe
- Stand close, touch frequently
- Moving back = rejection

### Moderate-contact cultures (0.8-1.2m)
- Eastern Europe, Asia
- Moderate distance
- Some touching OK

### Distant-contact cultures (1.2-1.5m)
- Northern Europe, USA, Japan
- More space preferred
- Less physical contact

## When Distance Changes

- **Crowded places**: Closer acceptable
- **Formal settings**: More distance
- **Intimate relationships**: Closer
- **Strangers**: More distance
- **Power dynamics**: Subordinate steps back

## Safe Practices

- **Observe locals** first
- **Mirror their distance**
- **Don't step back** suddenly (feels rude)
- **When in doubt**, give more space
- **Adjust based on context**
