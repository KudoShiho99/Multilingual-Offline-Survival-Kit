## Gender Rules

| Context | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Same gender | Generally OK in most cultures |
| Opposite gender | Avoid in conservative areas |
| Business | Keep professional |
| Stranger | No touching |

## Cultural Patterns

### High-touch cultures
- Latin America, Southern Europe, Middle East (same gender)
- Frequent touching during conversation
- Moving away = rejection

### Low-touch cultures
- East Asia, Northern Europe
- Minimal physical contact
- Touching may feel invasive

## Safe Practices

- **Observe locals** first
- **Don't touch head** (universal rule)
- **Right hand only** for touching in Muslim/Hindu areas
- **Match their touch level**
- **When in doubt**, no touching
